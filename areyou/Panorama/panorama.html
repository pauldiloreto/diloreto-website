Alfedena Panoramas Taken in 1946 
Antique Postcards from Alfedena, Italy.
Early 20th Century DiLoreto History
 
On leave with the U.S. Army in April 1946, Gilbert DiLoreto (1918-1998) visited Alfedena and took these panoramic photos, climbing the surrounding hillsides. Click on each image to enlarge. We welcome your comments at DiLoreto.com.
