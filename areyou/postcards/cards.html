Antique Postcards from Alfedena, Italy

Some of these postcards were sent from Alfedena between 1913 an 1923 to relatives living in Detroit and Rochester. Others were brought to America as souvenirs when family members migrated to the U.S. Click on each postcard to enlarge it. If you read Italian, you can make out some of the messages to loved ones overseas.

Hope you enjoy...and tell a friend or relative about DiLoreto.com. If you have questions, contact us.
