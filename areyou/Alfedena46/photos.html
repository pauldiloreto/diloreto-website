Panoramic Views of Alfedena, Italy
High-quality images taken in 1946

While still stationed in Europe with the U.S. Army after WWII, Gilbert DiLoreto (1918-1998) visited relatives in Rome and in his parents' birth town of Alfedena, when these pictures were taken in April 1946. Do you recognize any of these relatives? If you do, contact us at DiLoreto.com, and we will add a caption identifying the person. Click on each image to enlarge.
