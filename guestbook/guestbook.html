Saluti a Tutti DiLoreto, My father Rocco Nicolo was born in Filetto, Province of Chieti in 1893. As most did then he went to Argentina for awhile; back to Italy and then to the United States landing in Boston. World War 1 veteran,worked as a mason(STONE). Married Pauline DeLucia whom he met in Boston; had 3 sons,Adio now 83, Mario deceased and Gildo now 76. Brother Adio has four generations on this earth, I have no children.We are both retired school teachers and I have been a professional vocalist and musician for 60 years. Would like to hear from you. I have been to Europe plus Italy many summers; still have first cousins in Bari on my mother's side. Ciao
