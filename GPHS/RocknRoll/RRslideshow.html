GPSHS Reunion Greetings Slideshow No. 4

From 1975 to 1998 John DiLoreto played with Bay Area musicians in countless bands, jam sessions and recording sessions on keyboards and vocals. Music ranged from original rock to Motown and classic rock in "Crossfire" to "As You Were" to "The Best Times."

From 1980 to 1985 home-based Silver Label Recording provided demo and record-production services to local musicians and theater groups.

This track of "Heatwave" was recorded with "The Best Times" in 1998 with John on keyboards and background vocals. More of John's music bio can be found at DiLoreto.com's About page.
