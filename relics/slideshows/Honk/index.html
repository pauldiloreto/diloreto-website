HONK!

West Valley Light Opera’s HONK! ran for 13 performances at the Saratoga Civic Theater from March 3 - 31, 2007, directed and choreographed by Geri Sauls, and starred Jeff Clarke as Ugly. Carolyn had the role of Duckling Billie in a cast of 20 adults and 4 children. Stage and promo shots are courtesy of Edmund Kwong.
