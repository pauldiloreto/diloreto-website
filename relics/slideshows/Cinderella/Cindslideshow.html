In his 6th show with Children's Musical Theater San Jose, Paul won the role of the King in Rogers and Hammerstein's "Cinderella," helping to lead a cast of 80 8- to 13-yr.-olds. Paul loved the comedic and singing role in which he had a chance to share the stage with some very talented performers, including Alison Alampi as the Queen, Anna Maria Perez de Tagle as Cinderella and John Sousae as the Prince. The show ran for six sold-out perfomances at downtown San Jose's Montgomery Theater. It was especially exciting for Paul to perform to his 6th-grade class and the 7th graders at St. Lucy School.
