Carolyn played the Velveteen Rabbit in her first show with Children's Musical Theater, leading a cast of 58 six-to-nine-yr.-olds for four performances at the Montgomery Theater in downtown San Jose. Carolyn's many hours of practicing paid off with a strong performance that took us by surprise. She immensely enjoyed her role and made many new friends in her cast. But it was a special treat when her orphan castmates from Annie came to see her show on Saturday night.
