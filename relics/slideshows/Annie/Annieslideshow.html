In Carolyn's second show with Saratoga Drama Group, she played the role of Kate, the second smallest orphan. The production ran for 15 shows over 5 weeks at the Saratoga Civic Theater. Carolyn won the hearts of many with her spirited acting, dancing and singing. A special treat was when her principal from St. Lucy School came to see her perform. "You're a real scene stealer," she said.
