DiLoreto Mountain Home
2825 Chumash Circle
Camp Connell, CA 95223
(209) 795-5733
Elevation 5,060 ft.

Backing onto open forest land in Big Trees Village, this well-equipped home blends mountain and modern living. With level driveway and walk-up access, a vestibule entrance opens to living and kitchen areas with vaulted pine ceilings, skylights and stone hearth. The main level includes the master bedroom, along with the second bedroom and full bath. Downstairs, a family room with woodstove opens onto a separate deck overlooking the forest. Also downstairs is an extra-large bedroom with full bathroom and sauna. Two spacious decks allow stretching out for barbeque, and relaxation. Features include:

  • 3 bedrooms, 3 baths, 2440 sq.ft., .31 acre
  • Queen-size brass beds in two upstairs bedrooms
  • Two queen-size beds and bunk bed in downstairs bedroom
  • Sleeps 10 (plus 2 on family room sofa-bed)
  • Modern kitchen with gas range and tile counters
  • Two decks with patio table, lounge chairs and barbecue grill
  • 32" TV with cable, VCR and DVD
  • Hi-speed wireless Internet access
  • Hot tub and sauna
  • Membership to nearby Big Trees Village Recreation Center
