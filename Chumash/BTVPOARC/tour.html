A Tour of the
Big Trees Village
Property Owners Association
Recreation Center

The Recreation Center is open from Memorial Day to Labor Day. It's open just on weekends until the third week of June and 7 days a week until Labor day. It is located near the intersection of Boards Crossing and Sierra Parkway. The parking lot is the second driveway from the intersection.

 

 

 

On the way there, you will pass the children's play area with swings slides and monkey bars.

 

 

 

When you walk up the incline to the gate, you will see a number of announcements of current activities and events. Just past the gate on the right is the sign in sheet. Enter your name and 6-126, which is the unit and lot number.

 
The first thing you will see is the lower pool. This smaller, warmer pool with a depth of 3ft. to 6ft. is a favorite of the smaller children. It usually opens at noon but check the daily schedule. Ping pong tables are just off to the right.
Just past the ping pong tables is the Snack Bar, which is usually open most of the afternoon. Small softserve ice cream cones are only $1.00.
The upper pool is a full-size 25-yds. with a diving board. Check the schedule for lap swim at certain times and for general swim at others. It is surrounded by umbrella tables and a large grassy area with some shade for poolside picnics.
Elsewhere on the grounds are tennis courts, basketball courts and shuffleboard, tucked away among large trees and abundant shaded picnic tables. More information is available at the Big Trees Village website.
